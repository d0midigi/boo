I'm the mindhackdiva. I'm a cyber and information systems security engineer, vulnerability management specialist, threat hunter, researcher, offensive security specialist and HBIC of HBSS. By night, I play in DVWAs, hack things, run 3-4 cybersecurity-related technical blogs, and am in the process of publishing my first book on Offensive and Defensive Active Directory Security Techniques for Ethical Hackers. I have been a cybersecurity professional with almost 20 years in the field. Ping me any time, I love to talk about all things cybersecurity.

What Is This?


With countless security guides, references, and resources scattered across the internet, it can be overwhelming to navigate through them and figure out which ones are actually worth your time. This space is built upon one of my key strengths in security: knowing where to look to find factual, meaningful information, and my documentation and note taking skills. This space is a curated collection of some of the best tools and resources I've come across in my journey through the world of cybersecurity.


With thousands of blogs and tools out there, it can be difficult to determie which ones truly meet your needs. My hope is that I can save you some time by sharing the lessons learned through my own trial and error, guiding you toward the right resources to help you grow your skills.


This guide aims to provide essential information when possible, while directing you to additional resources that can further enhance your expertise.
